Ingredients Pizza Dough:
 - 3 3/4 cups flour
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
 - 1 1/3 cups warm water
 - 1 tablespoon sugar
 - 1 packet yeast
 - 3 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
 1. Whisk 3 3/4 cups flour and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt.
 2. Make a well and add 1 1/3 cups warm water, 1 tablespoon sugar and 1 packet yeast.
 3. When foamy, mix in 3 tablespoons olive oil; knead until smooth, 5 minutes.
 4.  Brush with water, cover in a bowl and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour 30 minutes. 
 5. Divide into two 1-pound balls. Use 1 pound per recipe unless noted.
